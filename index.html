Part IV: Testing




Objectives


Is the navigation easy to understand?

Do users feel they have all the information they need to adopt their pet and schedule a visit?




Task 1: Find a Basset Hound


Is it easy to find the right pet?

Can users navigate our filters and intuitively understand our layout?

Task 2: Fill Out the Adoption Form


Is the adoption form feel complete and easy to understand?

Does the wizard help users anticipate steps & add clarity to the process?




Task 3: Schedule a Virtual Meet


Is it easy to schedule an appointment using the calendar?

Do users want to see a pet virtually?

Results

  • From initial user testing, we observed that users wanted to see more options during the browsing stage & have more clarity of steps throughout the adoption flow. To remedy this, we increased the size & amount of our pet cards per page, placed additional info on the cards, and re-labeled our buttons throughout the adoption process to hint to our user what the next step will entail.
  • The results were overall positive towards the usability & style of the revised website, with users generally enjoying the soft, playful tone of the design language we employed through our style guide & components.
